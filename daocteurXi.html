Rise of Xi

小学博士是如何炼成的

Born son of the revolutionary hero Xi Zhongxun, Xi enjoyed a spacious home with dedicated cook and driver, all rare privileges at the time.

童年时代，父亲习仲勋是中共革命英雄，习近平过着特权生活，培养成为“太子党”。

-凯瑞

1966

Amid the social upheaval of the Cultural Revolution, Xi's father was purged from power, publically humiliated, and imprisoned.

文化大革命开始, 习仲勋受到清洗，被关进监狱,家人也受到株连

“我当时只有14岁。红卫兵问我，‘你知道你的罪行有多严重吗？’”

“我说，你给我估计估计，够不够枪毙？”

“他们回答说，’够枪毙100次。’”

“我想，100次和1次没什么区别。都100次了还怕什么？”

-习近平

I was only 14 at the time. They asked me:' Do you comprehend the gravity of your offences?

'Will I be executed?' I asked back.

'Your crimes warrants execution 100 times.'

I thought to myself, there is no difference between 1 and 100 times. What is there to fear on the 100th time?

-Xi Jingping

“那时候，我们只能喝粥，吃野菜和馒头。饥不择食。习近平没有幽默感。他不打牌，也不和其他年轻人出去闲逛或是找女朋友。 他（习近平）读毛泽东选集和毛主席语录以及报纸。当时也没有其他可读的。”

-吕侯生,梁家河农民

At that time, all we had for food are rice porridges, wild vegetations, and steamed bums. We would eat anything. I don't recall Xi having any sense of humour. He neither played cards nor hung out with people. He didn't date either. All he did was reading the newspaper and Mao's select. There wasn't anything else to read back then.

-Lv Housheng, farmer in Liangjiahe

1969

习近平潜回北京，被公安逮捕，羁押10个月后遣送回梁家河

Xi returned to Beijing on his own initiative, and was arrested by police. He was jailed for 10 months and deported back to Liangjiahe

1974

Xi's 10th application to joing the Chinese Communist Party was approved
习近平第十次入党申请通过

1975

习近平作为工农兵学员返回北京就读于清华大学，被分配至化学系
Xi returned to Beijing as a 'worker-farmer student', and was admitted to Tsinghua's chemical engineering department

1979

习近平随耿飙历任国务院办公厅、中央军委办公厅秘书（现役）

Xi became secretary to Gengbiao, then vice premier and later chief secretary of the Central Military Commitee

1979

习近平与中国驻英国大使柯华之女柯玲玲结婚

Xi and Ke Lingling, daughter of the Chinese ambassador to the UK, married

1979

习近平与柯玲玲离婚,柯玲玲移民英国

Xi and Ke Lingling divorced, and the latter immigrated to the UK

1982-85

习近平任河北省正定县委副书记,书记，正定县武装部第一政委、党委第一书记

Xi became a county-level official in Hebei

1985-88

福建省厦门市委常委、副市长

Deputy mayor of Xiamen

1987

习近平和彭丽媛在交往9个月后结婚

Xi and Peng Liyuan dated for 9 months and married

1988-90

福建省宁德地委书记，宁德军分区党委第一书记

Mayor of Ningde, party secretary of Ninde military base

1990-96

福建省福州市委书记、市人大常委会主任，福州军分区党委第一书记

Mayor of Fuzhou, chief of city council, party secretary of Fuzhou military base

1993

进入福建省委常委

Membership in Fujian provincial permanent leadership comittee

1995-2002

福建省委副书记,南京军区国防动员委员会副主任，福建省国防动员委员会主任，高炮预备役师第一政委

Deputy party secretary of Fujian, deputy chief of Nanjing military district national defence mobilisation commitee, chief of Fujian provincial national defence mobilisation commitee, chief of artillery reserve division

2000-2002

福建省长

Premier of Fujian

2002

在职研究生班学习4年后,获清华大学法学博士学位

Awarded doctorate in law by Tsinghua university after 4 years of remote study

2002-2007

浙江省委书记、省人大常委会主任，浙江省军区党委第一书记

Premier/party secretary of Zhejiang, chief of provincial parliament, deputy chief of Nanjing military district national defence mobilisation commitee, chief of Zhejiang provincial national defence mobilisation commitee, party secretary of provincial military district

2007

上海市委书记，上海警备区党委第一书记

Mayor of Shanghai (city with province status), party secretary of Shanghai defence zone

2007-12

中央政治局常委、中央书记处书记，中央党校校长

Standing commitee member of Chinese Polibutoro, secretary of the central secretariat, principal of the central party college

2008-13

中华人民共和国副主席

Vice chairperson of China

Since 2012

中央委员会总书记

Chief secretary of the central party commitee

Since 2013

中华人民共和国主席,中央军事委员会主席

chairperson of China, chief of the Central Military Commitee

Since 2016

彭丽媛任中央军委干部考评委员会专职委员

wife of Xi, Peng Liyuan, became a full-time member of the central military personnel commitee
